Typography

The Typography that I have chosen to be displayed on this website is Bodoni Moda, and Noto Sans JP. The first of the two fonts, will be used mainly for the headings of the website, or anything that will label and signify an important new subject. The latter, will be used to make up the bulk of the website's readable content. I chose these two because the one is serif, and the other is sans-serif, yet they both share a couple of qualities that make them match, while still showing distinct distinctions. I also chose them because the heading font is easy to read whilst still appearing important, and the main paragraph font shares that important feel, but with more lightheartedness.
