Site Name

weatheractivites.com

Site Purpose

This website aims to provide a weekly overview about weather conditions, in your local area. Unlike just looking up the weather on your phone, this website also provides additional information such as ideas of what can be done within this type of weather. The information it would provide would mainly be for re-creational and personal enjoyment reasons, such as is the current weather sweater worthy, or nice to play soccer in etc. The website would aim to also provide additional ideas or activities that one might be able to enjoy with the current weather that is displayed within the week.
