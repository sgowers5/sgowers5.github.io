Persona 1

Ficitonal Name:

Joanna Smith

Occupation:

Stay at home mom.

Demographic:

29 years old. Takes care of their two kids while their spouse goes to work and goes to school. Comes from a large family. Holds a registered nurse license.

Goals:

Wants to take her children to the park and enjoy outdoor play, but needs to see what the weather is so she can correctly dress herself and her children for the conditions. Also looking for additional park activites.

Technology:

She has access to a PC at home, but mainly employs her Iphone for her day to day needs.





Persona 2

Ficitonal Name:

Milo Stone

Occupation:

Fantasy Book Author.

Demographic:

35 years old. Likes to explore outdoors to gain inspiration for writing his books. Often goes hiking and out on camping trips for extended periods of time. Masters of creative writing degree.

Goals:

Planning a backpacking trip and needs to prepare for what the coming week will bring. Has to decide what to pack, in order to enjoy comfortably a time away from home.

Technology:

He has an android that he uses to keep in touch with his family and uses it for business needs. Also as a durable laptop that he takes everywhere he goes in order to write whenever inspiration strikes.
